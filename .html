Overview

The Shri Ksetra Scheme for Youth is a government initiative designed to promote youth engagement in cultural preservation, social development, and skill-building. The program aims to connect young people with India’s spiritual and cultural heritage while creating new pathways for employment and leadership.

Objectives

  • Encourage youth participation in cultural and community programs.
  • Support skill development aligned with heritage-based vocations.
  • Foster leadership and entrepreneurship among rural and urban youth.
  • Promote awareness of traditional Indian values and sustainable living.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for training and projects.
  • Certification from recognized institutions.
  • Access to workshops, mentorship, and internships.
  • Networking with professionals and cultural organizations.

How to Apply

Interested candidates can apply online through the official Shri Ksetra portal. The process includes:

  1. Register on the official portal.
  2. Fill in personal and educational details.
  3. Upload necessary documents.
  4. Submit and track your application status.
